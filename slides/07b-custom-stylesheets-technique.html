Example: custom stylesheets

Technique

  • More difficult to emulate in Figma, due to the open-ended nature of custom styles
  • Use Firefox's → View Page Style → No Style option to turn off author styles entirely
  • Use the browser inspector for ad hoc page style changes, or an extension like Greasemonkey for persistent changes
