What are user styles? (cont'd)

  • Lean into these differences instead of resisting them.
  • Meeting users where they are respects their choices, needs, situation, and autonomy.
  • Authors can guide, but users decide.
  • "User agents" are true "agentic" tools. Browsers and devices work on behalf of the user to accept, deny, or reformat content according to the user's needs.
