Example: forced colors mode

Good practices

  • Transparent or same-color borders
  • Border + shim for modals
  • Check focus/selected/etc states
  • Re-color icons
  • Check both light and dark forced-color themes
  • Be careful of misleading / ambiguous solutions (e.g. underlining text)
  • Force original color only when essential (e.g. color swatches for product variants)
