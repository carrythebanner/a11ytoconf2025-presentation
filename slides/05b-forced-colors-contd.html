Example: forced colors mode

What it means

  • It's a misnomer that it's one single "mode," or that it's just for "high contrast." Forced colors mode is a set of rules for how elements will be customized, but each person using it can choose their own presentation.
  • Specific colors aren't guaranteed, so you have to think about user intent and how to make your design malleable rather than designing for exact palettes or presentations.
  • Think along the same lines as supporting different screen sizes fluidly — not just at exact, specific breakpoints.
