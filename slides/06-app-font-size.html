Example: app font size

Good practices

  • Assume that users will change font size and appearance (e.g. bold text)
  • Proactively consider in which direction/s content will grow or reflow.
  • Think beyond just "phone" and "tablet" and consider split screen display, widgets, and more
  • In Figma, use auto-layout liberally and stress-test it
