What are user styles?

User styles are choices that the user makes which impact how a webpage or app is displayed.

  • Often this is a setting, preference, or other modification that a user makes
  • Could also be something inherent to the device being used: screen size, touchscreen or no, etc.
  • The user isn't necessarily making their actual best or preferred choice. They might be choosing the "least worst" option.
